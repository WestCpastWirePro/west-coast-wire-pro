// FAQ

Common Questions.

Which exam does this prepare me for?
The California General Electrician (Journeyman) certification exam administered by PSI Services on behalf of DLSE/DIR. Questions are based on the 2020 NEC and California-specific codes (Title 8 Cal/OSHA and Title 24 CEC).
Is this the actual exam content?
No — these are original practice questions written by working electricians to cover the same topics and skills the real exam tests. The actual exam questions are proprietary. Think of this as the best way to build genuine understanding, not just memorization.
What NEC edition is used?
The 2020 NEC (NFPA 70), which is the current adopted edition in California. Module 11 specifically covers California amendments (CEC), Title 8 (Cal/OSHA), and Title 24 requirements.
Do I need a code book to use this?
No. Every question explanation includes the relevant NEC article or section, so you know exactly where to look if you want to go deeper. The app is self-contained.
Is this a subscription?
No. One-time payment, yours forever. No recurring charges, no expiration dates. Study at your own pace until you pass.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. West Coast Wire Pro is fully mobile-optimized. Study on your phone during lunch, on a tablet at home, or on your laptop — it works on all of them.
